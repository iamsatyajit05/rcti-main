About RCTI

Welcome to R.C. Technical Institute, Ahmedabad. The R.C. Technical Institute was established by Rao Bahadoor Ranchhodlal Chhotalal (The First Nagarpati and pioneer of textile industry in India) in year 1910 at Saraspur,Ahmedabad. Later in the year 1930, this institute was handed over to the State Government of Bombay.


Initially, the institute was running certificate courses in Mechanical, Electrical, Textile Spinning, Textile Weaving and Textile Processing. In the year 1947, these certificate courses were replaced by new Diploma programmes in Textile Manufacturing and Textile Processing.


Looking to the need of industries, the programs like Printing technology, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, IT Engineering & Civil etc are added gradually. To accommodate this expansion in environmentally clean and layer area, the institute is shifted to the new campus at sola, Ahmedabad in June 1997.

About Computer Department

Diploma in Computer Engineering (C.E) encompasses a variety of topics that relates to computation, like analysis of algorithms, programming languages, program design, software, and computer hardware. In R.C.Technical Institute, Computer Department is established in the year of 2000.


Here we use learning tools like lecture-cum-discussions, laboratory works, tutorials, home assignments, seminars, field visits, finishing school trainings and project works to effectively teach curriculum designed by GTU. The department has well qualified faculties to cater the academic and other kinds of students’ needs.


Department possesses classrooms and prime laboratories for language programming, database designing and networking as per AICTE norms and standards. We offer Interactive and effective learning environment to the students.
